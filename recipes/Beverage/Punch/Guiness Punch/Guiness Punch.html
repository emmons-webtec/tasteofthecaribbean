Guinness Punch

A nice refreshing 'chill-out' drink over ice on a hot day! Guinness punch has a lovely 'rich' (as Caribbean folks would say), full flavour, that moves smoothly over the tongue. An easy one to make - all you need are the ingredients and a jug!

 

INGREDIENTS

 

1 bottle or can of Guinness

3 scoops ice-cream or 1 can of vanilla Supligen/Nourishment

½ cup of condense milk (some folks use a small tin)

1/2 tsp nutmeg

1 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp vanilla

(add more spices if desired, to your preferred taste)

2 cups crushed ice (optional)

 

METHOD

1. Add Guinness, ice-cream (or Supligen/Nourishment), and condense milk in a

         jug and mix well to combine.

2. Add nutmeg, cinnamon, and vanilla, and stir lightly.
3. Add crushed ice or place in a refrigerator to chill.
4. Serve cold and enjoy!
